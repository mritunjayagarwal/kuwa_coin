Our Project

KUWA is a friendly, affectionate and loyal dog that is different from any other dog. Kuwa loves to run and play in the sun. A furry and fuzzy being, Kuwa is more loving than he appears and loves like no other dog could. Found by his caretaker, Haru, as a small and abandoned puppy, Kuwa grows up to become a lovable and loyal aid to his side. He becomes the perfect companion for Haru to take along in all his journeys, becoming his only source of support in difficult times. Whenever Haru faces a tough time in his life, he knows he can count on Kuwa to lift his spirits high and Kuwa does just that. Haru nourishes him with care and affection in return for his loyalty and companionship.​

Kuwa was founded to spread the love and adoration of canines to people all around the world. Our love for dogs, especially Chikuwa is what led us to introduce Kuwa to the world. Kuwa is inspired by Chikuwa from the famous Japanese anime Yuru Camp. He is a symbol of the friendship, energy and resilience of Chikuwa from Yuru Camp.
