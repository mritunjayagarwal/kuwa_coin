Our Project

KUWA is a friendly, affectionate and loyal dog that is different from any other dog. Kuwa loves to run and play in the sun. A furry and fuzzy being, Kuwa is more loving than he appears and loves like no other dog could. Found by his caretaker, Ena, as a small and abandoned puppy, Kuwa grows up to become a lovable and loyal aid to his side. He becomes the perfect companion for Ena to take along in all his journeys, becoming his only source of support in difficult times. Whenever Ena faces a tough time in his life, he knows he can count on Kuwa to lift his spirits high and Kuwa does just that. Kuwa nourishes him with care and affection in return for his loyalty and companionship.​

Kuwa was founded to spread the love and adoration of canines to people all around the world. Our love for dogs, especially Chikuwa is what led us to introduce Kuwa to the world. Kuwa is inspired by Chikuwa from the famous Japanese anime Yuru Camp. He is a symbol of the friendship, energy and resilience of Chikuwa from Yuru Camp.

GET STARTED

1. Create Trustwallet or Metamask Wallet

Create a Trustwallet or Metamask wallet using either a desktop computer or a iOS/Android mobile device. That will allow you to buy, sell, send and receive KUWA.

2. Send ETH to your wallet

You can swap USDT into ETH or transfer it to your Metamask wallet from exchanges like FTX, Binance, Coinbase etc. Make sure you use ERC20 network when transferring ETH.

3.Open Uniswap and Connect wallet

Once the ETH has been deposited into your wallet, open uniswap.org and click connect wallet.

4.Swap ETH for KUWA

Press “Select a token” and enter the token address or search for it on the token list. Swap ETH for KUWA and benefit from our ecosystem.
